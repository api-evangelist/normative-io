Normative has launched Decarbonization Plans, a new feature that helps companies execute against their climate targets by providing one source of truth where emissions data and reduction initiatives live in the same platform. The tool addresses a critical gap: while over 10,000 companies have set science-based targets, only 67% are tracking progress on scope 1 and 2 emissions and just 54% for scope 3. By embedding decarbonization planning directly into carbon accounting software with clear accountability structures, the feature enables teams to transform aspirational climate commitments into operational reality.
